Some of Delaware's Trails

Valley Garden Park Loop

One of the best little-known parks of Delaware, Valley Garden Park's paved loop is beautifully maintained with a meandering creek and small waterfall in between rolling hills.

Gordons Pond Trail

A costal trail where you can walk down miles of wildflower-adorned trails that weave through dunes and wetlands that showcase natural and historical sights.

Jack A. Markell Trail

This 7.9 mile paved trail travels between the Wilmington Riverfront and Historic New Castle's Battery Park throug wetlands, over the Christina River and through forests and fields.

Swamp Forest Trail

A 7.5 mile loop around the largest pond in Delaware.
